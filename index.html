About Me

Hi there, welcome to my site! I'm currently in the process of developing my programming skills to eventually work in the tech field as a programmer, and this site serves as my portfolio. I realized at the end of my schooling that I didn't have enough projects to showcase to people, and decided to change that by creating this website to house all the projects I worked on, and to encourage me to work on projects to build up my portfolio.

I graduated from university with a Bachelor in Health Sciences in 2023, majoring in Bioinformatics. Throughout my degree I focused mostly on the programming aspect of my degree (rather than the biology aspect) and realized that programming is something that I can do and that I really like doing! Although I'm ready to get my hands dirty with any aspect of programming, my overarching goal is to create something that people can use, and especially something that can help people.

Below, you'll find links to projects that I've worked on/am currently working on. These projects fall under one of three categories; Personal, School, and Online projects. Once you click on a link, you'll be taken to a page that provides more details about the project, and if appropriate, you can even interact with it!
