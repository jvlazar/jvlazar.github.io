About Me

Hi there, welcome to my portfolio!

My name is Jessica and I'm a passionate full-stack developer, combining my love for the visual aspect of front-end work with the logical mechanics of the back-end.

I graduated from university with a Bachelor in Health Sciences in 2023, majoring in Bioinformatics. Throughout my degree I focused mostly on the programming aspect of my field and realized that programming is something that I can do and that I really like doing! Although I'm ready to get my hands dirty with any aspect of programming, my overarching goal is to create something that people can use, and especially something that can help people.

I have also done art as a hobby for the past 10+ years, and while I'm not a graphic designer I recently discovered that I highly enjoy the process of working on front-end projects. In combination with my desire to create something that can help people, I hope to work in both front-end and back-end development in my career to achieve this goal.
